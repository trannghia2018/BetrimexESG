Mission

EMPOWERING Vietnamese Coconut Value Chain

Betrimex is dedicated to empowering Vietnamese coconut value chain by championing green agriculture driven by cutting-edge technology. We strive to meet the highest standards of food safety, nutritional value, and traceability transparency. By advancing a high-tech, circular agriculture model that minimizes emissions and waste, we are deeply committed to striking a balance between economic growth, environmental protection, and social responsibility.

BETRIMEX AT A GLANCE

Beyond being a producer, Betrimex is a provider of leading innovative solutions, driving a fully integrated coconut value chain that maximizes the potential of every part of the coconut - from water and meat to shell and fiber. Rooted in a strong “Zero Waste” commitment, Betrimex has redefined what’s possible: transforming waste into value, accelerating circular economy practices, and delivering sustainable benefits for both people and the planet. This model not only enhances the livelihoods of communities in Bến Tre but also positions Betrimex as a strategic force for long-term, inclusive growth.

Product safety and quality

Betrimex is dedicated to delivering safe, high-quality, international-standard products that follow the philosophy: “Quality and reputation are Betrimex’s top priorities”. To fulfill this commitment, Betrimex applies advanced traceability technologies, enforces strict production controls, and upholds multiple sustainability certifications. Responsible sourcing and product traceability

Betrimex focuses on building and developing a network of strategic suppliers who declare a long-term commitment to ESG standards, with particular attention to those involved in packaging that comes into direct or indirect contact with products. In addition to obtaining essential certifications (such as FSC, ISO 9001:2008, and SA 8000) as entry requirements, these suppliers are required to integrate ESG principles strategically and commit to long-term alignment in both production and operations
